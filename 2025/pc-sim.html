In-game Websites

Here are all of the hidden websites that are currently known, and what they do.

www.giveaway.com

This website has been once used for giving away 30 Bitcoins in the PC Simulator Discord server.

www.darknet.com

Named after the illegal marketplace, this hidden shop can be used to buy¹ the Super Glue and the PUOIO Phone.

¹ While they do have price tags, they will not deduct from your in-game money.

www.giveaway.com

Website for free coins! Just kidding, when you click that Claim button, the computer you are on will explode. This is a less expensive version of the Used SSD bomb.

Save Editor

This Python-built save editor can decrypt and save files for the game, it can likely work with future versions.
