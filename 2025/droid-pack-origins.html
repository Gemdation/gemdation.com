Our story starts...not in 2024, but in 2013! I saw a few animated YouTube videos that led to a whole website being made. In this blog I'll talk about how Droid Pack came to be in late 2024.

The First KitKat

During 2013, I saw these promotional videos for Android KitKat. I really loved the Bugdroids shown, I honestly wished they were in a cartoon or something.

I should also mention that I liked the jellybean easter egg that appeared in the Android Jellybean, the predecessor to KitKat.
