Smartphones

HTC Phone

Behold, the first smartphone I ever used. It was used for playing Angry Birds, creating my first Google account, and creating my (now deleted) Facebook account.

This phone had issues with:

  • Game audio
    • I remember one time Angry Birds kept playing music after I closed the game and that scared me enough to remove the battery
  • Battery percentage
    • If the battery happened to be pulled out while the number was at 100% and I put it back in and rebooted, it would be at 40%.
  • Anything involving the Google Play Store
  • Wi-Fi at time would disconnect
  • Being fragile in general
    • I was very upset one time when this phone was 100% and I dropped it on a hard wood floor in my grandma's house, rebooted it, and saw that it went back to 20%

    Image is not the exact model, I do remember it was from 2014 (guessing by the Facebook account's date) and it did not have Android 3.0 or newer.
