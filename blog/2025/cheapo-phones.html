Cheapo Phones

I'm gonna go over my last phones I remember having! And I'll end with the one I am typing this on now.

2017-2018

Samsung Galaxy J7 V

One of the more earlier phones I can remember using is a Galaxy J7, this was one of many Samsung devices I have owned. This also had a brief experience with Samsung Cloud that stored childhood drawings.

LG Aristo 2

I really like the design of this phone out of most that I've had, it had a smooth back and a fingerprint reader. 16:9 screen as well! I remember playing Pou on it and installing a modified APK to get more coins.

2019-2020

Samsung Galaxy J7 Star

This phone also ended up getting lost but before that it had a nice life, complete with mobile data! It had a sparkly finish similar to a Nintendo 3DS and the camera was decent.

Samsung Galaxy J2 Core

Tiny cute little phone! It was bought with another J2 as part of a bundle. This phone I liked because of it's regular screen size, which was good for YouTube videos and old mobile games. My mom's J2 ended up breaking so I gave her mine and that ended up getting abandoned and I ndidn't know wherelost.

2021

Nokia Lumia Icon 929

This was a regretted eBay purchase after I found out that Microsoft Accounts can't be used on it anymore, and getting it's APK equivalents installed were a hassle. I did enjoy playing some games on it when I was in the ninth grade. The apps on it looked nice but were ridiculously underpowered, the Internet Explorer app loaded worse than a console browser.

2023

iPhone 5c

This blue iPhone was jailbroken by me and used solely to play some iOS-exclusive games, at times I treated this as a little game console and just had it in my pocket. One thing that bugs me is that the Socket exploit is not permanent and will not work after it gets restarted.

Not to mention the out-of-date iOS, I already don't like iPhones so this was a bad experience. This is also the only iPhone I have and when I forgot the password to the Apple ID I almost thought I locked myself out of it. I then reset it from the device and not the website and, yeah. iPhone.

Sony Xperia Z5 Premium

Premium as in the old premium. This was a(nother) regretful ebay purchase after I found out that Japanese phones can't work on American networks. I would've been fine with that if it could run games without heating up the back glass like an electric stove. It was also too old for some apps on the Google Play Store.

2024

Vortex HD65 Plus

This phone is so wack I can't even find a page for it, let alone an official one. Anyway this phone was very big and while I did like my old Note 3 this was just to big to fit in my pocket. The touch screen on it also weird and will hallucinate taps.

BLU View 4

This phone is clearly not the fastest but far from the slowest ones I had. I don't like the back of it though, it looks like it is from a COD game. And the anything-but-16:9 and camera placement are my pet peeves with all smartphones.

I use it to watch YouTube videos, chat on Discord, and play some games while I'm at school. (fun fact I am writing this at school right now) Sadly this phone can't handle videos with 60FPS or else it'll tone the video down to a slideshow, and mobile games run fine enough.

The problem is the long display crops games weirdly and I have a bunch of old Pac-Man mobile games on here that have weird borders when I open them. Some don't even open at all. (Pac-Chomp! and Pac'N-Jump!)
