Memey

December 31, 2024

In a more simpler time, I liked browsing through r/Memes on Reddit and subscribing to meme channels on YouTube. If you've heard of Grandayy, Memetastic, SMG4, and whoever else. This was also during the time when the whole PewDiePie and T-Series war thing was happening.

These are not exactly something I look back fond on, like some people. I remember one time I was being a hater at the cafeteria because one kid used Instagram for memes instead of Reddit. I do like the rest of the nerdy people I hanged out with, they were cool.

Grandayy

Definitely a product of it's time, Grandayy was not really someone I paid attention to but I liked some of his videos. One of his collaborations for a fan-made YouTube rewind was cool. Later on I just didn't like his content, a simple sad ending.

Memetastic

Basic meme regurgitator, but it's not just that. I can't mention Memetastic without his edgy Discord server with quite the toxic fanbase who ironically hated him.

Videos exposing him were blocked and staff were power hungry. It doesn't stop at the outside of the server, nothing does. Memetastic unknowingly started a hurd of spin-off servers and Discord trolls with his server, like me!

PewDiePie

It's been 5 years since I've watched any of these YouTubers, but if there is one that really needs to be here, it's him. Meme Review and Reddit were a big chunk of my humor back then and I'm glad it was, I wouldn't want it to be TikTok and Khaby Lame. Also his little Minecraft series was cool, I liked how it gave a slight revive to the community.

Hobo Bros

Before the storm! SMG4 was one of my favorite channels and even though I didn't like it later on I did like Hobo Bros. My favorite video of theirs was of them playing Super Mario Dolar. The cursed commercials reactions were cool and made me like reactions videos again.

I don't plan to watch these YouTubers but it is nostalgic to remember what my humor was back then. Have a dank new year.
