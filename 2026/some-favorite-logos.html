Movie Studios

I actually like most of these. The best haven't changed (Disney and Pixar), some have had neat 2D transformations (Warner Bros.), some just look better in 3D (20th Century Fox, Universal Pictures), and some of them are just too oversimplified (21st Century Fox).

Most of these are noticeably monochrome, probably to make it not focus on one specific color.

TV Networks

Quite extensive. And most of the logos haven't changed much from when I was a kid. All of these are nice, I don't have much negative comments apart from the ones that were changed to monochrome.

Retail

Colorful and mostly unchanged! Walmart has had a recent simplification that works a bit and Best Buy has a simplification that...fits in with the 2020's, I'll say.

While I do love the conveinence of the big two (Walmart and Amazon), I still like the concept of catered retail.
