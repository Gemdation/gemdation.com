YouTube is and will be forever my favorite platform for entertainment. It started way back in 2014, possibly earlier but I'll pay tribute to the first one I remember watching.

[hotdigdemon's fnaf vid]

A horrific but fun start for a few neat reasons, the first one being it was from an 'edgy' creator in his prime. Entertaining and led to some more people but faded away in the 2020s. That animation led to a neat channel called EthGoesBOOM, who had made a 'reaction' video while voicing FNAF character Bonnie and Foxy.

At first I was wondering why the video wasn't playing, and then I heard two voices. These were Eth's interpretations of Bonnie and Foxy and they gave comments throughout the video. I thought it was a nice concept and that led to something else. AnimeBroMii
